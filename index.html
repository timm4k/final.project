The definitive fan site resource for Riot Games' competitive 5v5 character-based tactical shooter, VALORANT, that was released on June 2nd, 2020. Set in a near-future Earth, you team up with 4 other players against 5 enemies in round-based combat with an agent of your choice. Creativity is your greatest weapon.

Valorant - is an online shooter from Riot Games that combines elements of strategy, speed, and accuracy. The game differs from other shooters with its unique system of heroes, each of which has its own unique skills and abilities.

Players have to choose the hero that best suits their playstyle and strategy. Each round in Valorant is a real battle of wits and reactions, where every move can decide the fate of the team. By playing Valorant, players can develop their skills, improve their strategy, and communicate with other players from around the world.
